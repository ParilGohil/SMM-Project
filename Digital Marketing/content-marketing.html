CONTENT MARKETING & BLOGGING

Content is King. Creating and distributing valuable, relevant content to attract and retain audiences — and ultimately drive profitable customer action.

// Definition

What is Content Marketing?

Content marketing is the practice of reaching potential customers through the use of content. Content is usually published on a website and then promoted through social media, email marketing, search engine optimization, or even PPC campaigns. The goal is to create content that provides genuine value to your target audience — content that educates, entertains, or solves problems. The tools of content marketing include blogs, eBooks, online courses, infographics, podcasts, and webinars.

// Video Content

Video Marketing

Using video for marketing means incorporating video format into your marketing strategy to promote a brand, product, service, or message. Video marketing began gaining traction in 2005 with the advent of YouTube, which Google acquired in 2006. The rise of smartphones made video consumption ubiquitous. During the pandemic, media consumption increased by 215% in the United States alone. Experts believe video outperforms all other forms of content for engagement and conversion.

// Strategy

Running an Effective Video Campaign

01
ALLOCATE RESOURCES

Budget for video equipment, editing software, dedicated time, and a team to bring all elements together professionally.

→
02
BUILD YOUR STORY

Every video tells a story. Define your message, what you want to convey, and how you want to share it to maximum emotional impact.

→
03
PLAN ENGAGEMENT

Hook your audience in the first 3 seconds. Plan how to keep the story interesting and the viewer vested throughout the full duration.

→
04
KEEP IT SHORT

The online audience's attention span is short. Shorter is better — edit ruthlessly. Remove anything that doesn't serve the core message directly.

→
05
PUBLISH EVERYWHERE

Embed on website, upload to YouTube, publish on all social media accounts. Saturation is the goal — promote it heavily across every channel.

→
06
TRACK RESULTS

Monitor metrics and stats. Identify what the most successful videos have in common and apply those learnings to all future video production.

Blogging & Blog Platforms

Blogging encompasses every activity and skillset required to manage a blog: writing posts, publishing them, designing the layout, social marketing, and more. In simple words, blogging is the process of adding new content to a blog and updating it at regular intervals. "Content is King" — and a blog is the primary vehicle for long-form content marketing. Bloggers can earn income, build networks, improve writing skills, and establish themselves as published authors in their field.

// Blog Growth

Blog Promotion Tactics

SEO Optimization: Write search engine friendly content with proper keyword targeting. This ensures long-term organic traffic from Google searches.

Social Media Sharing: Share every post across all social platforms. Use social media for branding. Connect blog to Google+ for auto-sharing.

Email Newsletter: Have an Email button for reader feedback. Use blog link in email signatures. Enable site feed for subscriptions.

Blog Directories: Add your blog to Blogger's listings. Submit URL to blog directories. Participate in online forums to increase profile exposure.

Guest Blogging: Write as a guest blogger on popular sites in your niche. This builds backlinks and exposes your content to established audiences.

Comment & Engage: Actively comment on others' posts. Participate in online forums. Hold contests or carnivals to generate buzz and new readers.
