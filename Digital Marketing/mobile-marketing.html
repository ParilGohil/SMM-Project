// Definition

What is Mobile Marketing?

Mobile marketing is a multi-channel, digital marketing strategy aimed at reaching a target audience on their smartphones, tablets, and other mobile devices via websites, email, SMS and MMS, social media, and apps. Mobile is disrupting the way people engage with brands. Everything that can be done on a desktop computer is now available on a mobile device — from opening an email to visiting your website to reading your content, all accessible through a small mobile screen.

// Strategies

Types of Mobile Marketing Strategies

📱

App-Based Marketing

Mobile advertising involving mobile apps. 80% of mobile time is spent in apps. Services like Google AdMob help advertisers create mobile ads appearing within third-party apps. Facebook Promoted Post ads integrate seamlessly into news feeds that users often don't recognize as ads.

AdMobIn-App
🎮

In-Game Mobile Marketing

Mobile ads appearing within mobile games as banner popups, full-page image ads, or video ads between loading screens. Reaches highly engaged audiences during gaming sessions when attention is focused.

Banner AdsVideo Ads

QR Codes

Scanned by users, taking them to a specific webpage. QR codes are aligned with mobile gamification and have an element of mystery — users don't always know exactly where scanning will take them, creating curiosity-driven engagement.

ScannableEngagement

Location-Based Marketing

Ads that appear on mobile devices based on a user's location relative to a specific area or business. Advertisers can target users within a 1-mile radius of their business with hyper-relevant, local offers.

GPSGeo-Fencing

Mobile Search Ads

Basic Google search ads built for mobile, featuring extra add-on extensions like click-to-call buttons and maps integration. Designed for one-thumb usability with immediate action capabilities.

// SMS Campaigns

Why SMS Campaigns Matter

📊

Extraordinary Open Rates

SMS campaigns achieve a remarkable 98% open rate and a 45% response rate — figures email marketing can only dream of. This makes SMS the highest-engagement marketing channel.

⚡

Drives Immediate Action

SMS messages create a feeling of time sensitivity and urgency. No other marketing channel creates such a strong urgency that many customers decide to act rapidly within minutes of receiving the message.

⏱️

Easy & Instant

SMS takes only moments to create compared to HTML-designed emails. 95% of all texts are read within 3 minutes after being sent, enabling real-time campaign results measurement.

🔧

Multifunctional

SMS campaigns can inform about new products, deliver exclusive offers, send updates and notifications, remind about appointments, report relevant news, or improve customer service.

🤝

Works with Other Channels

SMS can improve email marketing results — send SMS to "catch up" people who never opened your email. Use it strategically as a supporting channel in omnichannel campaigns.

// App Marketing

Mobile Apps

A mobile app is a software application developed specifically for use on small, wireless computing devices such as smartphones and tablets. Mobile apps are designed with consideration for device demands and constraints, and also to take advantage of specialized device capabilities. A gaming app, for example, might leverage the iPhone's accelerometer. App marketing strategy involves both driving downloads (ASO) and retaining active users through push notifications, in-app messaging, and personalized experiences.

// App Store Optimization

App Store Optimization (ASO)

App Store Optimization (ASO) is the process of optimizing mobile apps to rank higher in an app store's search results. The higher your app ranks, the more visible it is to potential customers — and increased visibility translates directly to more traffic to your app's page and more downloads.

📛

App Name / Title

Place the keyword with the heaviest search traffic in the title. Avoid changing it too often — as your app ranks higher and gains reviews, word spreads. Changing title disrupts organic word-of-mouth growth.

Primary Factor
🔑

Keywords

Know which keywords are most relevant and frequently used by your target audience. Monitor competitors weekly to understand how you compare and identify keyword opportunities.

ResearchMonitoring
📝

Description

Clearly communicate what your app does and its benefits. The first 3 lines are crucial as users must tap "More" to see additional content. Include social proof and key features prominently.

First Impression
⬇️

Downloads & Ratings

Total downloads and ratings significantly impact ASO ranking but are difficult to control directly. Incentivize happy users to rate and review the app through in-app prompts at the right moments.
