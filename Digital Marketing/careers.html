// More Roles

Other Digital Marketing Roles

✍️
Content Writer / Strategist
Creates blog posts, articles, web copy, and content strategies. Requires strong writing skills, SEO knowledge, and audience research abilities.
📧
Email Marketing Specialist
Designs and manages email campaigns, manages subscriber lists, segments audiences, and analyzes performance metrics like open rate and conversion rate.
📊
Digital Analytics Specialist
Uses Google Analytics, data visualization, and tracking tools to translate raw data into actionable insights. Guides optimization decisions across all digital channels.
🎥
Video Content Creator
Produces YouTube videos, Reels, and video ads. Requires videography skills, video editing software expertise, and understanding of platform-specific optimization.
🤝
Affiliate Marketing Manager
Manages affiliate programs, recruits partners, tracks performance, and optimizes commissions. Builds relationships between merchants and affiliate publishers.
🎯
Digital Marketing Manager
Oversees entire digital marketing strategy, manages multi-channel campaigns, leads teams, and ensures all activities align with business objectives and budgets.
// Learning Path

Skills to Build

Google Analytics: Essential for tracking website traffic, user behavior, conversion goals, and campaign performance across all digital channels.

Google Ads: Master the world's largest PPC platform. Create search, display, shopping, and video campaigns with proper targeting and optimization.

Facebook Ads Manager: Learn audience targeting, campaign structure, A/B testing, and retargeting on the world's largest social advertising platform.

Canva / Adobe: Visual content creation skills for social media graphics, presentations, infographics, and basic design work that elevates brand aesthetics.

WordPress / SEO: Website management with SEO plugins like Yoast. Understanding on-page optimization, content management, and technical health monitoring.

Email Platforms: Mailchimp, HubSpot, or Klaviyo for creating, automating, and analyzing email marketing campaigns at scale.
